Chris' Analysis on the type of League players

League of Legends is a team-oriented game which typically involves 10 online players on a single battlefield. The purpose of this game is to destroy the opposing team's "Nexus", the home base of a team.

There are many different skill levels of players in this game. Below is a table documenting the percentage of players out of over 1.6 million total in North America at varying skill levels:

Ranks Bronze Silver Gold Platinum Diamond Challenger and Master
Percentages 14.09% 47.83% 28.73% 7.48% 1.8% 0.04%
# of people 226,021 768,120 461,514 120,234 28,895 642
Hours played a week 0 - 2 0-4 (varies) 1-6 6-15 10-20 30+

By showing the different skill levels of people playing League of Legends, it will give people an idea on what kind of individuals play this game. For example:

  1. People in Bronze, Silver or Gold are usually students or adults with little free time.
  2. People in Platinum are students or adults with quite a bit of free time.
  3. People in Diamond are individuals who have 0 stress in their lives.
  4. People in Master or Challenger are full-time professional League players.
